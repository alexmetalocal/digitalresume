Leveraging Your Market Value in Negotiations

Armed with your OneCrate salary estimate report, you're equipped to effectively negotiate your job offer. Begin by presenting your findings to your prospective employer, highlighting how your expected salary aligns with industry standards and market trends. Emphasize that your expectations are rooted in data, rather than arbitrary figures.
